About SRMCEM

Shri Ramswaroop Memorial College of Engineering and Management (SRMCEM) is a premier institution dedicated to academic excellence and holistic development. Established with a vision to create future leaders, SRMCEM offers world-class infrastructure, highly qualified faculty, and a vibrant learning environment.

Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, skills, and ethical values, enabling them to excel in their professional and personal lives. With a strong emphasis on innovation, research, and industry collaboration, SRMCEM is committed to nurturing talent that can make a positive impact on society.

Located in Lucknow, SRMCEM boasts state-of-the-art laboratories, a well-stocked library, modern classrooms, and a campus that encourages both academic pursuits and extracurricular activities.
